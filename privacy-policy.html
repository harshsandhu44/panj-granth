Privacy Policy for Panj Granth

Effective Date: January 2025

Overview

Panj Granth is a mobile application for reading Guru Granth Sahib. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

Panj Granth does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using local storage:

  • Reading History: Angs (pages) you have read
  • Bookmarks: Angs you have bookmarked with optional personal notes
  • Settings: Your preferences for translations and app appearance

This data is never uploaded to any server and remains completely private on your device.

Third-Party Services

Panj Granth uses the following third-party service:

  • Gurbani Now API (https://api.gurbaninow.com): Used to fetch Guru Granth Sahib text, translations, and daily Hukamnama. The API does not require authentication and does not collect personal information. Only the requested page/content is fetched.

Network Permissions

The app requires internet permission (INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE) solely to:

  • Fetch Guru Granth Sahib text from the Gurbani Now API
  • Fetch daily Hukamnama from Golden Temple

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

Data Security

All your data (reading history, bookmarks, notes, settings) is stored securely on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms. This data is only accessible by the Panj Granth app.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Clearing bookmarks individually within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (which removes all local data)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
